Football academy

Soccerkids football academy will be running development centres in conjunction with Charlton Athletic's Youth Academy

The head coach will be Nicky Potts. Nicky has spent over 15 years working in Youth Development and has been involved in Grassroots football for over 20 years.

He has worked with several players currently player in the English premier league as well as the football league

The centres hope to attract young players and will be initially concentrating on players in School years 1 up to year 4, currently.

Players will be assessed and if they are ready will be invited into the academy at Sparrows Lane for an Official trial.

This kind of practise will help players develop and assist players to improve their performance when representing their clubs. Regular communication with parents will take place through our communication platform and regular arranged fixtures will take place throughout the time spent with the development centre. These will also take place during the school holiday period.
